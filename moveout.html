Selling Your Home!

Get Ready To List

Selling your home can be a daunting process! From fixing that squeaky stair you've lived with for years to figuring out how to price your home, there are a lot of steps in the process of selling your home, and it can take a lot of time and energy.

Homesellers are facing higher expectations than ever. Today's buyers expect a smartly-pric home, expertly staged rooms, and more natural lighting than a photographer's studio. But with help from me and the I will provide you with a checklist guide; we'll have your home sold in no time!

What Is The Value of Your Home Based On?

There are several factors that go into finding the Market Value of your home. Read below to see what I use to price your home.

  • Today's Market
  • Today's Competition
  • Today's Financing
  • Today's economic conditions
  • The buyer's perception of it's condition
  • The Property location
  • The normal marketing time

There is also a number of things the Market Value of your home is not based on. For example:

  • The contents or investment you have in the property
  • The amount you need out of the property
  • The amount you want for the property
  • The appraised value of the property
  • What you heard your neighbor's house sold for
  • How much it is insured for
  • Home prices in the area where you are moving to

Home Selling Timeline

Wondering what the timeline looks like for selling your home?

Initial Interview & Property Tour
An Initial Interview and property tour is conducted.
Consulation
  • Evaluate property's Condition
  • Determine initial terms of sale
  • Establish an asking price ( to be reviewed and / or updated regularly )
Sign Listing Paperwork
  • Decide on the repair / fix ─ up items, I have a list of vendors to help you get this done if some items are overwhelming or you are not in the area!
  • Photography and staging
  • Prepare the home for showings
Marketing Period
  • Install a lock box and yard sign
  • Enter the property data in the MLS
  • Advertise the property online through portals such as HowardHanna.com , Realtor.com and many others.
  • Create and distribute property flyers
  • Mail ' Just Listed ' postcards
  • Schedule an Open House
Buyer Presents Offer, Negotiations Begin
  • We review the offer together
  • A Seller's Net Sheet is provided to show what the offer means to your financial bottom line
  • We strategize on whether to counter the offer or accept the terms as presented
  • Responding promptly is critical
  • Once negotiations are agreed upon, the contract is considered ratified
Pending Period
  • Any inspections required by the contract terms are now orderd
  • Repair items are noted and / or negotiated
  • You are then responsible for completing any repairs agreed upon
  • Just before closing, the buyer conducts a final walk ─ through to check on repairs and to confirm the property is in the same condition as when the offer was initially made
Settlement
  • Conducted by a closing agent - may be an attorney or a settlement agent from a title company
  • All required documents are signed
  • The property transfer is recorded
  • Funds are disbursed immediately or within two business days depending on the location of your property
